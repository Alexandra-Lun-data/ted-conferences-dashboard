# Insights

## Engagement Patterns
Audience engagement is highly concentrated: a small number of talks generate the majority of views and reactions.

## Topic Impact
Certain topics consistently outperform others in terms of applause and laughter.

## Duration Effect
Shorter talks tend to have higher engagement levels compared to longer ones.

## Speaker Influence
Speaker occupation plays a significant role in audience response.

## Practical Use
These insights can help:
- choose better topics
- select speakers
- design conference structure
